Quest Adventure Camp

Quest Adventure Camp, is the place that you can do all the activities like team building, school activities, and more.

Tarzan Swing

The Tarzan swing is fun, but you need to be careful not to fall into the water. When I go to the camp again, I would love to try this activity again.

Giant Volleyball

In this activity, you need to be careful because you might get injured. The ball is so heavy that you need to form a team, and you guys need to play as a team.

Trust Fall

Also, this activity can be nerve-wracking because you don't know if your team will be able to catch you. And you need six people to play this activity.
